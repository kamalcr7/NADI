NADI

About NADI

NADI (meaning "Pulse" in Bahasa Melayu) is an open-source, all-in-one data portal designed to track the vital statistics and real-time updates of Malaysia.

Our Mission

We believe that open public data is key to civic participation, tourist utility, and expat relocation success. By converting raw data feeds from the Malaysia Open Data APIs into premium, fluid-animated, interactive visualizations, NADI empowers citizens and visitors with clear, actionable insights.

Data Integrity

All information displayed on this website is pulled directly from official government endpoints:

  • Ministry of Health (MOH) — Dengue Surveillance
  • Department of Statistics Malaysia (DOSM) — Population, GDP, Inflation, Labour Force
  • Malaysian Meteorological Department (MET) — Weather Forecast & Warnings
  • Bank Negara Malaysia (BNM) — Daily Exchange Rates
  • Ministry of Domestic Trade (KPDN) — PriceCatcher Consumer Goods

NADI is an independent project and is not officially affiliated with the Government of Malaysia.
